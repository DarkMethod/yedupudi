FAQs

Why would I pick you over my friends and relatives?

As we all feel the age old method of falling back on friends and relatives to do the job for you does not work as efficiently in modern times and often causes a rift between relations. Everyone wanted to have their tasks managed by the subject matter experts. Yedupudi employs qualified personnel to do the homework for you, thus saving your family the trouble and stress of running from pillar to post.

How do I register for the service ?

To register with our company you can create a free Yedupudi account using 'Sign Up for a free account' button at the top. You can also sign up with us using either your Google or Facebook account at the Sign Up page.

Where can I get application forms?

All services can be registered online on our website.

Do I have to file application form online?

Yes. Application process is done online through our website.

Can I make copies of the application forms for filling out and mailing it out?

We maintain a paperless application process. Hence it will be mostly via online. We require some supporting documents such as proof of ownership and authorization for services like property management.

No. The application process is done online.

The fees are associated with service enrollment only.

Do you take credit cards?

Yes. We honor all major credit cards. Payment can be made through Paypal.
